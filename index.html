About Us

Welcome to Hotel Waikiki, your luxurious escape in the heart of paradise. Situated on the breathtaking shores of Waikiki Beach, our hotel offers unparalleled views of the Pacific Ocean and Diamond Head volcano.

At Hotel Waikiki, we are dedicated to providing our guests with an unforgettable experience. Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, family vacation, or business trip, our attentive staff and world-class amenities ensure that your stay is nothing short of perfection.Indulge in our spacious and elegantly appointed rooms, savor exquisite cuisine at our onsite restaurants, and relax by our sparkling pool surrounded by lush tropical gardens. Discover the true essence of aloha at Hotel Waikiki. Experience the magic of Hawaii with us and create memories that will last a lifetime. Mahalo for choosing Hotel Waikiki as your home away from home.
